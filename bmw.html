BMW:-

Luxury Vehicles maker BMW India Private Ltd. is a wholly owned subsidiary of BMW, Germany.
BMW India was founded in the year 2006 and is headquartered in Chennai. In March 2007, the
company's Chennai production facility started production of BMW cars in India. The company
manufacturers BMW 3 Series and BMW 5 Series cars at its Chennai plant. BMW cars in India are
sold through its well equipped dealer network and after sales services are provided by state
of the art BMW service centers in India.
