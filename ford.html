FORD:-

Ford India is a wholly owned subsidiary of Ford Motor Company of United States. Ford is the sixth
largest car maker in India. Ford Motor company entered the Indian automobile market way back in 1926.
However, they had to close down their manufacturing facility in 1954 owing to the socialist policy
of the then government. Ford later re-entered Indian market in 1995 as the Mahindra Ford India Ltd following a 50-50 joint venture with Mahindra and Mahindra. Later Ford increased its stake in
Mahindra Ford and renamed the company to Ford India Private Limited. The company's manufacturing
plant is located near Chennai in the state of Tamil Nadu where it also houses it headquarters.
Ford Dealers Ford Car Models Ford offers 5 models in the Indian market. The most popular Ford
cars in India are Ford Ikon, Ford Fiesta and the recently launched hatchback, Ford Figo. The price
of Ford cars in India starts from Rs. 363000 for the Ford Figo Petrol Model. The Ford Endeavour SUV
with a price of over Rs. 1974000 is the most expensive Ford car available in India. Ford India has
over 323 sales and service network spread across 26 states in India.
