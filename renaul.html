RENAULT:-

The entry of French automobile maker Renault into Indian passenger car market was a result of a 51 - 49 Joint Venture between Mahindra
& Mahindra Limited and Renault S.A in the year 2007.
Mahindra Renault has a state of the art car manufacturing facility at Nasik in the state of Maharashtra. As of now Mahindra Renault
offers only one car model in India called the Renault Logan. Mahindra Renault Logan cars are sold through Mahindra and Mahindra Dealers in India.
Mahindra Renault Dealers Mahindra Renault
Car Models Logan is the only car marketed by Renault in India.
Though Logan is a right-hand drive car manufactured for the Indian market, many of its features are just like that of a left-hand drive car.
This has invited lots of criticism and negative reviews by the Logan Model.
The price of Logan car is between Rs.464000 and Rs. 669000 in India.
