HYUNDAI:-

Hyundai India is a wholly owned subsidiary of the South Korean automobile manufacturer, Hyundai
Motor Company. Hyundai is the 2nd largest car manufacturer in India after Maruti Suzuki. Hyundai
Motors India Ltd. was established in the year 1996 and in September 1998, Hyundai launched it first
model, Hyundai Santro in India. Entry of Hyundai into the Indian market ended the monopoly of Maruti
Suzuki in the passenger car segment. Hyundai Santro was a huge success in India and within few
months of its inception, Hyundai became the second largest car manufacturer in India. Hyundai India
is headquartered in Chennai.
The company has a state of the art production facility near Chennai and also a research
and development facility in Hyderabad.
Hyundai Dealers Hyundai Car Models In India, Hyundai sells 11 models of cars which include
hatchbacks, Sedans and SUVs. Hyundai Santro Xing with a price starting at Rs.269999 is the most
popular Hyundai Car selling in India. Other Hyundai Car models sold in India are Hyundai i10 and
i20, Hyundai Accent, Hyundai Verna, Hyundai Sonata and Hyundai Santa Fe. Hyundai has one of the
strongest and most extensive sales and service network in India after Maruti Suzuki. Hyundai has
over 396 dealers spread across 32 states and union territories in India.
