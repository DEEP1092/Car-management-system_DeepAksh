CHEVROLET:-

Chevrolet India is a division of General Motors India Private Limited which inturn is a
subsidiary of the United States automobile giant General Motors or GM. Chevrolet first entered
Indian automobile market in 1928 and continued production till 1953, before they were asked to
leave India by the then government. Later on in 2003, Chevrolet re-entered Indian market and is
currently the 5th largest car maker in India. Chevrolet India is headquartered at Halol in the
state of Gujarat where it operates one of its manufacturing facility. Chevrolet has another
manufacturing plant at Talegaon Dabhade in Maharastra and also a large technical center in
Bangalore. Scroll down to know more about Chevrolet India Cars, Chevrolet Car prices in India and
chevrolet car dealers in India.
